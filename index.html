Learn how you could get a fresh box of cute cat hats each and every month!

We all love our cats and want to make sure they always feel as special as they are! That's our goal at Cat Hats Monthly. That's why when you sign up we will send you a fresh box of goodies for your cat each and every month!

Here are some of the things you can expect from your monthly box:

Cat Hats

Four adorable hats for your cat to wear and look super cute in.

Toys!

Two cat toys for your cat to play with. Each month's toys will be fun and unique!

Yummy treats

A bag of grain-free and healthy cat treats.

Extra surprise!

And the part your cat will probably like most... it all comes in a cardboard box that has been sprayed with a catnip mist for them to play in.
